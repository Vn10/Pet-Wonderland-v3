❓ How to Play

Everything you need to know

1
Create your pet
Choose a Dog, Cat, or Dragon and give them a name. Your pet starts with balanced stats and $150.
2
Watch the stats
Your pet has four key stats. Keep them healthy!
Hunger — goes up over time. Feed your pet to reduce it.
Happiness — keep it high by playing and cleaning.
Energy — depleted by playing. Restore by resting.
Health — drops if you neglect your pet. Vet restores it fully.
3
Earn money
Visit the Earn tab to play mini-games. Whack moles for $1 per hit, or test your reaction speed for up to $10 per round.
4
Buy toys in the Shop
Better toys give your pet more happiness when you play. Upgrade from the Rubber Duck ($15) → Chew Bone ($75) → Ultra Ball ($200).
5
Unlock Badges
There are 8 badges to collect. Reach milestones like max health, high happiness, or earning lots of money to unlock them all!
💡 Pro Tips
• Rest is free — use it often to save money
• Clean regularly to keep health up without vet bills
• The reaction game rewards $1–10 depending on speed
• Mole hits = direct dollars, so keep clicking!
Start Playing 🚀
